About Me

I'm Dhanvan Kumar, a passionate and driven Full-Stack Developer and AI/ML aspirant, currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science at NIMS University, Jaipur.

I specialize in building robust and scalable web applications using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), and have a solid grasp of Java programming. My problem-solving skills are sharpened through continuous practice in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), making me confident in writing efficient and optimized code.

Apart from web development, I am deeply curious about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and I am actively exploring innovations in this field. I also have a growing interest in Cloud Computing technologies, which I believe are shaping the future of scalable applications.

I am always eager to learn new tools and technologies to expand my skill set and stay updated with the latest industry trends. My goal is to contribute to impactful projects and solve real-world problems through technology.
